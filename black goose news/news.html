Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food(usually vaccum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is pricisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In this book, Cooking for geeks,Jeff Potter describes it as ultea-low-temperature poaching.

Next month, we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at the chef table, contact us before November, 30.
